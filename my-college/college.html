UnderGraduate

The full form for UG is undergraduate. Undergraduate refers to the studies done after completing schooling (post-secondary education) and before attaining a postgraduate degree.

PostGraduate

PG stands for postgraduate, representing the subsequent higher education level available to students after completing their undergraduate program.

Phd

PhD is short for Doctor of Philosophy. This is an academic or professional degree that, in most countries, qualifies the degree holder to teach their chosen subject
